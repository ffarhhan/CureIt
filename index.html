Hi, I’m Subham. Nice to have you here.

I am a certified fitness professional and functional nutrition coach with over 8 years of experience in Karate. My journey in fitness began as a martial artist, where I developed discipline, resilience, and a profound appreciation for the connection between physical and mental health. Over time, I expanded my expertise to include functional nutrition, blending fitness and dietary strategies to promote sustainable well-being.

Having personally struggled with and overcome health issues like sinusitis, hypothyroidism, and eczema, I know firsthand the challenges of living with chronic conditions. These experiences shaped my approach to self-healing and fueled my passion for helping others on their health journeys. I firmly believe in addressing the root causes of illnesses and providing tailored solutions that empower individuals to reclaim their health and vitality.

I earned a Fitness Coach certification from the renowned K11 School of Fitness Sciences to further my knowledge and credibility. Today, I use my professional skills and personal experiences to guide others in transforming their lives. Whether it’s through personalised fitness plans, nutrition coaching, or encouragement, I am dedicated to helping people achieve their health goals and live their best lives.
